Welcome to My World of Music

Hello! I'm Coast. Growing up in Birmingham, I was surrounded by soulful tunes that set the stage for my lifelong love affair with music. Early in college, I fell head over heels for the beats—and despite a brief stint as a dentist (yes, I once fixed teeth!), I knew my heart belonged behind the DJ booth.

I like to think that while I might have learned to handle drills, nothing compares to the thrill of dropping the perfect beat. I'm all about keeping things fun, humble, and a little cheeky—because life is too short for boring playlists!
