Who I Am

Welcome to My World of Music

Hello! My name is Ian. Growing up in Birmingham, I was surrounded by soulful tunes that set the stage for my lifelong love affair with music. This love affair created Coast — a DJ who specializes in all forms of music from House, Top 40's, to a wide range of genres that will have you Coasting all day or all night long.

There is nothing compared to being behind the DJ booth playing for music lovers just like me. My taste provides for the main stage club, small private events, and even weddings.

I'm ready to Coast… Are you?
